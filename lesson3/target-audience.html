Target Audience

People of all ages seeking to go on a vacation. Families looking for a runaway trip to spend the holidays. Retires looking to see the world before nature calls them. Couples planning a surprise trip for their spouse. This site is generally for those of a working age that can pay for a trip to a new place they haven't been to.

Personas

Persona 1. The Lucas family is a middle class american family looking for where to spend the holidays, a special get away place where their children can relax from the rigors of school while learning of of the rich African culture and people. The Lucas family wants a to know they're in safe hands, that the locations they're going to is safe for family visits.

Persona 2. Hailey is a Canadian student majoring in photoraphy. She wants to see the world and take instructive and breath-taking photos of the world, and she wants to start with Africa. SHe hopes to tell a story none other has told before, in a way few have thought of. She wishes to explore the people, culture, food, and sites of the Southern Nigerian people.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find a list of Hotels or a place to stay in Southern Nigeria?
  • Where is the best destinations for relaxation?
  • I want to compare Hotel deals with in Nigeria with that of my hometown to know if the charges are moderate
  • Which flights can take me to Southern Nigeria from my hometown?
  • What is the culture and people of southern Nigerai like? How's their food? How do they dress?
